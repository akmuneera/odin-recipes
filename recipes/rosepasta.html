Rose Pasta


Description

Rose pasta is so easy to make!

You only need a few ingridents and it will be on of the most addicting recipes

Ingredients

  • Tomatoe
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Seasoning of choice
  • Pasta of course!
  • Cream
  • ,and tomatoe sauce

Steps

  1. Add oil or butter to pan
  2. Add onions until they become clear
  3. Add tomatoes until they melt down
  4. add tomatoe sauce, cream, and seasoning
  5. mix everything together
  6. add your pasta and enjoy!
